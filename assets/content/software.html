Fortunes

While in college I made a "game" called Fortunes using C++ that generates a detailed "fortune cookie" of your future life

I began writing it in early college and had finished it about halfway through.

It was a great experience, I learned a lot, I even used world census data for names and divorce rates for the randomizer.

When I took some more advanced classes I started to rewrite it because I realized how much it could be improved.

I still have a lot of work to do on it, but it has been a great learning experience.

Word Norms

In 2012, I worked with the Human Factors Lab at the University of South Dakota on their word norms research.

I made some tooling to help input and analyze significant quantities of textual data,
and these programs were used by more than half a dozen people who were working on this project.

The analysis tool was made in C++ and the input tool was made in Visual Basic.net
